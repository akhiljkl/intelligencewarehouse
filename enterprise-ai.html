QUESTION 2

Build or Buy?

The short answer: Buy the infrastructure. Build the agents.

Here's why. Every enterprise AI agent needs three things to make a correct decision: entity resolution (what am I looking at?), metric computation (what do the numbers say?), and decision rules (what should I do about it?). Today, each team builds this from scratch for every agent — extracting tribal knowledge, hard-coding business logic into prompts, rebuilding context pipelines. It works once. It breaks at scale. The infrastructure underneath the agents — the ontology, the metrics, the decision logic — is the same across all of them. Build that once, and every agent becomes a thin query layer on top.
